“In my art, the human being occupies a central place — their true essence, life principles, emotions, interaction with the world, and the beauty reflected within them. Therefore, my work combines figurative, abstract, surrealistic, and stylized elements.

I employ various styles and techniques, discovering new forms of self-expression throughout the process — this defines my artistic language. I enjoy exploring different directions, mastering new techniques, and working with diverse materials — oil, acrylic, ink, watercolor, charcoal, pencil, canvas, cardboard, and paper. Through this exploration, I expand my creative boundaries and seek the unknown. I hope this journey inspires others to discover their own unique artistic voice.

I often begin with pencil sketches and drawings to shape my ideas. This process helps me find forms that resonate deeply with my inner state and reflect my present emotional condition. These sketches guide my choice of technique and artistic approach.

When working with colored ink, I proceed intuitively — starting from a single point, spot, or line without knowing where it will lead. An inner force transforms into the desire to create. By deconstructing objective form, I build an abstract resonance of a specific image. In this process, I trust spontaneity and the element of chance.”
